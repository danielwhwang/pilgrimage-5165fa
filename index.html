★ Tier 1 — top banger

Lalibela, Ethiopia — Bete Giyorgis (Church of St. George)

Gateway: ADD (Addis Ababa) → Lalibela • Reach: Ethiopian Airlines via Aeroplan (UR + MR)

An entire cross-shaped church carved down into solid rock ~900 years ago. Almost nobody on your feed has this. Add white-robed Orthodox priests and it's the most distinctive image on the whole list.

The shot: Stand on the upper rim looking down into the cross — you small at the edge, the church glowing below. Osmo Pocket 3 for the wide drop.
★ Tier 1 — top banger

Meteora, Greece — Monasteries on the Pillars

Gateway: ATH (Athens) → ~4 hrs to Kalambaka • Reach: many UR/MR partners to Athens

Eastern Orthodox monasteries perched on sheer rock pillars, mist swirling below. Looks computer-generated. Pairs with a "Paul's footsteps" Greece trip (Athens, Corinth, Patmos).

The shot: Golden hour from the road viewpoints — you on a foreground ledge, a monastery floating on its pillar behind. Mist days are the magic.
★ Tier 1 — top banger

Cappadocia, Turkey — Balloons & Cave Churches

Gateway: IST (Istanbul) → ASR/NAV (Kayseri/Nevşehir) • Reach: Turkish via Aeroplan/United (UR + MR)

Byzantine cave churches carved into the rock at Göreme — and the famous sunrise where 100+ hot-air balloons fill the valley. Folds right into a "Seven Churches of Revelation" Turkey trip. Your highest-odds banger.

The shot: Sunrise on a cave-hotel terrace, you with coffee, balloons rising behind. Insta360 for the ascent if you splurge on a flight.
Tier 2 — iconic but heavily shot

Petra, Jordan — The Treasury (Al-Khazneh)

Gateway: AMM (Amman) → ~3 hrs to Petra • Reach: via Aeroplan/partners. Pairs with Mount Nebo & the baptism site.

The rose-stone Treasury revealed through the slot canyon — a genuine jaw-dropper, just very photographed. The biblical-history pairing (Nebo, Bethany-beyond-the-Jordan) is what makes the trip. You called this one out — recognizable, photogenic, definitely a cool shot.

The shot: Stand small at the base for scale, or frame it through the dark Siq canyon walls (the "Indiana Jones" reveal).
Tier 2 — iconic but heavily shot

Jerusalem — The Old City & Dome of the Rock

Gateway: TLV (Tel Aviv) → ~1 hr to Jerusalem • The Holy Land core: Via Dolorosa, Western Wall, Galilee, Bethlehem.

The gold dome over the Old City at golden hour is the signature Jerusalem frame. Deepest significance of anywhere on the list; the photo is classic rather than novel.

The shot: Mount of Olives overlook at sunrise — you in the foreground, the gold dome and Old City walls catching first light.
Tier 2 — iconic but heavily shot

Hagia Sophia, Istanbul — Byzantine Interior

Gateway: IST (Istanbul) • Reach: Turkish via Aeroplan/United (UR + MR). Easy to combine with Cappadocia.

1,500 years of Christian then imperial history under one dome — gold mosaics of Christ, soaring arches. A crowded indoor shot, but the mosaics are unmatched.

The shot: Stand small beneath the main dome looking up, or by the gold Deesis / Madonna mosaics on the upper gallery.
Tier 2 — iconic but heavily shot

Vatican / St. Peter's, Rome

Gateway: FCO (Rome) • Reach: best award space of the bunch (United, Aer Lingus, Flying Blue, etc.).

St. Peter's Basilica and Bernini's colonnade — early-church history (catacombs, Peter & Paul) plus the grandest square in Christendom. Magnificent, but the "everyone has this" shot.

The shot: Centered under the obelisk with the colonnade sweeping around you, or the basilica at blue hour from Via della Conciliazione.
