Lalibela, Ethiopia — Bete Giyorgis

The shot: Stand on the upper rim of the rock trench; the cross-shaped church sits carved into the ground below and behind you.

Meteora, Greece — Monasteries on the Pillars

The shot: Foreground ledge at a road viewpoint; a monastery floats on its rock pillar behind you.

Cappadocia, Turkey — Balloons & Cave Churches

The shot: Cave-hotel terrace, 30 min before sunrise; balloons rise behind you over the fairy chimneys.

Petra, Jordan — The Treasury (Al-Khazneh)

The shot: Stand small on the open ground in front of the facade for scale — or frame it through the dark Siq.

Jerusalem — Old City & Dome of the Rock

The shot: Mount of Olives overlook or an Old City rooftop; the gold dome and walls catch the light behind you.

Hagia Sophia, Istanbul — Byzantine Interior

The shot: Stand small beneath the central dome and look up; or by the gold mosaics on the upper gallery.

Vatican / St. Peter's, Rome

The shot: Stand on the marble “Centro del Colonnato” disk — the four rows of columns line up into one around you.
